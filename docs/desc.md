Cross-modal Content-based Retrieval for 3D Medical Images

by Inria
Your Challenge

Content-based retrieval in medical imaging can help clinicians find related patient cases by searching directly from image appearance rather than metadata alone. Build a brain MRI retrieval system that matches each scan to the corresponding scan of the same patient taken with a different imaging protocol. At training time, you are given paired training examples from a well-aligned dataset. At testing time, your method must generalize to datasets where the paired images are deformed, misaligned, or structurally changed.
